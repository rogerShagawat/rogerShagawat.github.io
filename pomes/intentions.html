roger shagawat
title: intention

i lift my toe into the air
drop it low cast off care
with a boom cats scurry sweep the broom in a hurry crumbs flying everywhere

perhaps it's time to come to grips
this lack of rhyme thunderous rips
tear through the very soul of a man
though i am inept with bowl and pan
i'll take those shrieks to heart
and touch down.

with swift row and far more care

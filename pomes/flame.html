roger shagawat
title: flame

tom takes the quarter out of his pocket and inserts it into the machine
“Honey let's go see that new movie”
his wife is standing there trying to get a stain out of her pink blouse
“I don't know where the theater is”
a can of coca cola is dispensed from the machine
“whose this for?”
his wife rubs her sponge against the blouse more vigorously now
“hello how can i help you?” the cashier says
“2 for Starry Night Flame”
the cashier hands tom 2 tickets each on an 8.5 by 11 sheet of paper
the stain won't come out
the couple sit down
they ordered 2 drinks and a large popcorn
“just like old times hey hun”
the stain still isn't coming out, it seems to be spreading on the blouse
a man in all green stands up and shouts “fire fire fire”
he points up at the projector
a faint glow of orange emanates from the projector room, smoke billowing out and going up through the chimney
the movie starts and tom wraps his arm around his wife whose to concentrated to notice
“tom did you turn the oven off”
tom turns around to see a face in the projector room
“what oven?”
“tom you've forgotten haven't you”
flames lick the face from top to bottom
“the oven tom”
the fire alarm goes off and people calmly exit the theater
tom takes a sip of his drink and a bite of his popcorn
the blouse is fully covered in the stain and the sponge is saturated with a purple liquid
“looks like we have the theater all to ourselves”
credits roll
“is that it?”
tom tried to read the paper in his hand but it's to covered in popcorn butter to make out any of the words
curtains close and the audience erupts in applause
tom sits silently waiting
the applause grows louder and louder
his wife joins in covered in a dark purple film which drips off her and forms a stream down the aisle
tom sinks low into his seat and covers his eyes
and they don't stop applauding
and they don't stop applauding
and they don't stop applauding…

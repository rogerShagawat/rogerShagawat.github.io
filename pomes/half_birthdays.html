2023-02-04
roger shagawat
title: half birthdays

what is my purpose here?
pondered in the back of this oft unvisited sauna
am i trying to escape even with this lack of trauma
cast to walk alone barely together
training for things that i never remember
is it applied science? this all encompassing solitude
why is there no purpose in life something to strive for
all our ancestors searching for food having everything to die for
not saying it would be easy i just wouldn't be so confused
i would cherish even my half birthdays because i wouldn't be fused
with the ground or those i can no longer access
striving for nothing including success
water splashes onto the coals turning to vapor
should i remain still or try to be written on paper
a flush from just beyond the door as a patron enters
we try not to look at each other as distance sits us apart
wafting from the only other door the stench of farts
i get up and leave

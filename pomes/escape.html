roger shagawat
title: escape

here i eat under this hulking blue mass
the currents stirring keeping strong and fast
couldn't ask for better
without task or tether
but OOOOUUUCCCHHH no longer in my COOOOUUUUUCCH
what predator is this
an otter? yet no prying from fists
perhaps a human? grasping for pearl with a frown,
but no, by now certainly i'd be drowned?
maybe a creature with jaws which crush any matter?
no, by now one of us would surely be shattered
the pressure grows and grows shell about to falter
i hadn't even considered
but it must be a gigantic ancient lobster!
struggle as i may i cannot match its claw!
but perhaps i can slip aw-
CRACK!
entrails burst from the old clam's shell
the lobster's grip crushing with a final spell
now to feast with mouth agape
a pearl comes bursting out to finally escape

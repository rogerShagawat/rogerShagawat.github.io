roger shagawat
title: I-80

here I am in this car stretched out on I-80
on all sides surrounded by my copies
fog so thick you could cut it
fog so thick we can barely see each other

I-80 begins in Albany California born from Route 101 in a manger between Trader Joes and REI
I-80 ends crushed between Teaneck and Ridgefield Park, crashing against the shores of Overpeck Park Playground, washing away as Interstate 95
2,545.4 miles separate me from it's birth 23.87 miles separate me from its death.
I have never seen either

here we are racing along its life at 70 miles per hour,
faster when we can, slower when sirens remind us not to.
we all have places to go. things to eat. people to be
we will never see each other's faces, learn each other's names,
no time for that.
and the fog won't let up.

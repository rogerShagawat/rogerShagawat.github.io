title: I forgot the salt

Prod and push, and push and pull.
Broth divine with white wine mull.
Diced the carrots soft but firm.
Potatoes and peas all a churn.
Fire hot liquid boiling in tune.
Set to simmer as I grab my spoon.

A taste to ease the buds...
Something's missing, tastes like mud.
Pantry flies spices gone to roost
Search and search but not produced
How could I do this how could I betray
I forgot the salt. GOD, WHY MAKE ME THIS WAY?!

It shouldn't be hard I'm not making curry
With bag in hand was in a hurry
The soup is ruined I must confess
Can't believe I went and made all this mess
Pour the soup down the drain
What a waste but I'm to blame

Dribbling down my face a tear falls into the pot
Flavor falling but the heart is set on not
Following through I can't do anything right
Now here comes that eternal fight
I could just buy more salt but it's too late now
This always happens just can't tell how.

2023-02-11 roger shagawat
title: wolf
theme: against a canvas

I think I would be a wolf,
candles licking at the page
to stop a bubbling brook
takes rocks and wood and wage

burning boiling magma
takes a more thought out touch
to stop its flowing rhythm
takes a careful thoughtful brush

but laborious endeavors
require organizational faculties
kings, pharaohs, prophets, emperors
old and tired majesties

the machine god on high
will direct every single one of us
to work and slave and die
by your next due date on canvas
